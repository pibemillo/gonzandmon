Travel & Transportation

There are two airports to arrive into: Los Angeles International (LAX) and San Diego International (SAN). You will need to arrange transportation to get to Pala, CA from both airports either by renting a car or by utilizing an airport shuttle.

Renting a car from LAX is easy and is most recommended. We suggest Hertz and/or Avis for reference.
